  • Age: 35
  • Occupation: Software Engineer
  • Location: Suburban setting, with easy access to local markets
  • Family: Married to Sarah (34), with a 6-year-old son named Ethan
  • Dietary Considerations: Sarah is lactose intolerant, and Ethan has a peanut allergy
  • Health Goals: Maintain a balanced diet, explore new healthy recipes, and ensure meals align with family dietary restrictions.

JOHN’S HEALTH ODYSSEY WITH “THE PANTRY CHEF”

John, a health-conscious software engineer, finds himself juggling the demands of a full-time job and the responsibilities of being a loving husband and father. Despite his busy schedule, he's committed to prioritizing the health and well-being of his family. Aware of his wife Sarah's lactose intolerance and son Ethan's peanut allergy, he's always on the lookout for nutritious yet allergen-friendly recipes.
One evening, after a particularly hectic day, John decides to take charge of dinner. Rather than resorting to the usual rotation of meals, he turns to "The Pantry Chef" app for inspiration
