HOW WE THINK

The Home page of the app features the “Recipe of the Day”, everyday these would update automatically, giving the user an instant answer to their need for a delicious option for “Breakfast”, ”Lunch”, “Dinner” and “Snacks”.

The very next icon next to the Home page is the icon for “Trending Recipes”, which gives the user a plethora of recipes to choose from.

Once you select a recipe to make from either the “Recipe of the Day” or “Trending Recipes” page, your recipe is displayed with further detail. The ingredients, nutritional details being on the top and an option to play the recipe while you prepare it, on the bottom, making it much easier to follow.

The app also has a “Weekly Meal Planner” that allows the user to plan their plans for the rest of the week. The app takes into consideration the user’s dietary restrictions and health goals while provinding a personalised and very user-centric experience.

While deciding your recipes for the rest of the week, the user can add ingredients to their “Shopping List”, that will remind them to grab any groceries they may be running short of.
